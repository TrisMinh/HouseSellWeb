# Rule: Completion Checkbox Sync

## Bat buoc

1. Lam xong task nao phai tick ngay task do trong `tasks.md`.
2. Lam xong phase/plan nao phai tick ngay o file `plan-xxx.md`.
3. Khong duoc de trang thai text `DONE/PENDING` ma khong co checkbox tuong ung.
4. Truoc khi bao cao ket thuc, bat buoc ra soat lai toan bo checkbox trong plan/task pham vi dang lam.

## Chuan tick

- Chua lam: `- [ ]`
- Da lam: `- [x]`

## Quy tac ket thuc

- Chi duoc ket luan hoan tat khi danh sach checkbox trong plan va task da duoc cap nhat day du theo tien do thuc te.
